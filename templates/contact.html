How to contact mates near you?

Via Email

One of the best solutions to contact somebody. Here are a few benefits of using email. Fast - an email should reach its recipient in minutes, or at the most within a few hours. Convenient - your message will be stored until the recipient is ready to read it, and you can easily send the same message to a large number of people. So be sure to include your email while registering.

Via Facebook

Second solution for contacting mates is via Facebook. Here are a few benefits of using Facebook. Fast - the faster you can communicate, the more people will trust. Free to use - facebook messaging is truly free. All you need is Internet access, and away you go. You can send files & images, make a phone call. So be sure to include your Facebook account while registering.
